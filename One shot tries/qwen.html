Why now?

Stablecoins are moving into regulated financial infrastructure

1
As PSPs and banks adopt stablecoins, the bottleneck shifts from payment execution to financial operations: liquidity, segregation, reconciliation, reporting, counterparty risk, and interoperability across fiat and digital assets.
2
Most infrastructure was built around transactions. The next generation of regulated money movement needs an account-level operating core.
3
Depa is built for that shift: one ledger for fiat, stablecoins, and eventually tokenized positions.
